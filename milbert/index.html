About MILLBERT BUSINESS SYSTEMS

Leading Engineering and Trading Company in Zambia

MILLBERT BUSINESS SYSTEMS Ltd is registered in Zambia as an engineering and trading company providing services in electrical, civil, and mechanical engineering through its engineering division, also commercial supplies to the mining industries. Incorporated in 2017, we have been delivering quality services across the Copperbelt and other provinces.
